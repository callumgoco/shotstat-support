Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Introduction

At ShotStat, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

ShotStat collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Shooting statistics and performance data
  • User preferences and settings
  • App usage data for improving the user experience

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide and improve the app's functionality
  • Generate statistics and insights about your performance
  • Personalize your experience

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store your data on external servers unless you explicitly choose to export your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Export your data
  • Delete your data
  • Opt out of analytics (if applicable)
