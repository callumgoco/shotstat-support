Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my shots?

Simply open the app and tap the "+" button to add a new shot. Enter the details of your shot, and the app will automatically calculate and update your statistics.

Can I export my data?

Yes! You can export your shooting data in various formats for further analysis or backup purposes.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We take data privacy seriously. All your data is stored locally on your device and is never shared without your permission.
